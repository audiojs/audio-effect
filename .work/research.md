## Goal

* Collection of canonic audio filters with complete flexible params set.
* Applicable in audio processing ops: from web-audio-api to audio.
* Open perspective of complex effects like neural adaption or stereo breadth changes - something cool VSTs sell.
* Ideal timeless result: collection of classic audio-effects, known params, well-described, well-demoed.
